# Office Rush 95
![Header](https://img.itch.zone/aW1nLzI1NTQ5NjQ5LnBuZw==/original/kEU0P5.png)
Office Rush'95 - Future Edition is a couch Competitive 3D game, where your goal is to be the better employee! Race against the time, complete tasks faster than your opponent and gather points, while pushing, blocking and shutting the door on your competitor.

<h2 align="left">Features</h2>

- NPCs that will waste your time if you get too close to them
- Pushing mechanic to throw your opponent off rythm
- 3 minigames that will make your time worthwile (Stamping, Sorting, Button Code)
- Keycards and closeable doors to trap your opponent or lock them out
- A beautiful office map
- 3 Playable characters
- ... and much more

<h3 align="left">Stamping Minigame</h3>

You'll be presented a paper with either 1 or 2 symbols on it, and 3 Stamps that are also colored in either Red, Green, or Blue. Your task is to stamp the symbol with it's corresponding Colored stamp, and after every symbol is stamped, to Drag and Drops it into the Tray right next to it. You'll have to repeat this a number of times until you're rewarded with a point.

<h3 align="left">Sorting Minigame</h3>

In this minigame, you'll have a stack of colored paper and 3 Trays that are also colored in Red, Green and Blue. You'll have to sort the correct paper into a Tray, until your stack is empty.

![Sorting](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1051801782818451479/1481508915345883330/Screenshot_2026-03-12_052441.png?ex=69b39232&is=69b240b2&hm=4164ae0fb580c56e7b6ef98f43da2c4cb3c02f314199a68f39e4e1f3c12cbadd&)

<h3 align="left">Email Minigame</h3>

You are just presented a Screen with a combination of Button codes. Press the buttons fast enough to not run out of time, or risk failing the task.
