# Office Rush 95
![Header](https://img.itch.zone/aW1nLzI1NTQ5NjQ5LnBuZw==/original/kEU0P5.png)
Office Rush'95 - Future Edition is a couch Competitive 3D game, where your goal is to be the better employee! Race against the time, complete tasks faster than your opponent and gather points, while pushing, blocking and shutting the door on your competitor.

<h2 align="left">Features</h2>

- NPCs that will waste your time if you get too close to them
- Pushing mechanic to throw your opponent off rythm
- 3 minigames that will make your time worthwile (Stamping, Sorting, Button Code)
- Keycards and closeable doors to trap your opponent or lock them out
- A beautiful office map
- 3 Playable characters
- ... and much more
